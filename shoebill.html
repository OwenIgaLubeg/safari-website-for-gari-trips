Shoebill Birding Experience in Uganda

Encounter one of Africa's most sought-after birds in its natural habitat. Join Gari Trips Uganda for an unforgettable adventure tracking the prehistoric-looking Shoebill through Uganda's wetlands.

Meet Uganda's Most Famous Bird

The Shoebill Stork is one of the world's most unique and fascinating birds. Known for its massive shoe-shaped bill, piercing stare, and prehistoric appearance, the Shoebill attracts birdwatchers, photographers, and wildlife enthusiasts from around the globe.

Uganda is regarded as one of the best places on Earth to see the Shoebill in the wild, making it a must-see species for any serious birding safari.

Best Places to See Shoebills in Uganda

Uganda offers several remarkable Shoebill habitats. Mabamba Swamp on the shores of Lake Victoria remains the country's most popular Shoebill destination due to its high success rate and accessibility from Kampala and Entebbe.

Other excellent locations include Murchison Falls National Park, the Albert Nile wetlands, and selected areas around Lake Kyoga.
